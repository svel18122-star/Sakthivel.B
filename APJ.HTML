APJ Abdul Kalam

He Is The Scientist Of ISRO

Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam was a pivotal scientist at ISRO (Indian Space Research Organisation), where he served as the Project Director for India's first indigenous Satellite Launch Vehicle (SLV-3). His leadership was crucial in the successful development and launch of the SLV-3, which placed India's Rohini satellite into orbit in 1980, marking India's entry into the "Space Club". He also played a key role in the evolution of the Polar Satellite Launch Vehicle (PSLV) program
